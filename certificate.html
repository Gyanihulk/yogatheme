Certification Guidelines

To receive your 200-hour yoga teacher certification, you must adhere to the following rules: complete all course modules, pass all assessments, and fulfill the required practice teaching hours.

Embark on Your Yoga Teaching Journey with Our 200 Hour Certification Course

This comprehensive certification course is designed to deepen your understanding of yoga and develop the skills necessary to teach yoga to others. Gain knowledge in asanas, teaching methods, and the philosophy behind the practice.

  • Understand the foundational principles of yoga teaching.
  • Learn effective cueing, sequencing, and student engagement.
  • Access to digital certificate upon successful course completion.

Our program is carefully structured to ensure that you not only learn the theoretical aspects of yoga but also gain practical experience through teaching practice. The course concludes with an assessment to evaluate your understanding and readiness to teach.

Upon completion of the course, you will be able to download your digital certificate, signifying your achievement and readiness to start teaching yoga professionally. Join our community of certified yoga instructors and share the benefits of yoga with the world.
